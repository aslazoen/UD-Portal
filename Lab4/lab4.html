ABOUT THE HOTEL

Chachagua Rainforest Hotel & Ecolodge is one of the most respected rainforest hotels in Costa Rica, where travelers can experience the country's amazing nature and biodiversity. Our ecolodge set in the northern plains by Arenal Volcano is a tribute to the beautiful land that we are fortunate to inhabit. Our on-site facilities, accommodations, and activities have been created with ecology, preservation, and exploration in mind.

At Chachagua, we are 100% committed to sustainable tourism and we strive to be environmentally responsible in everything we do. Our first-class facilities, adventures and activities, on-site eco-specialists and organic farm operations are intertwined naturally with our tropical surroundings. We have created our experience to give each guest an unforgettable and authentic immersion with nature, without sacrificing the quality and luxury that every traveler deserves.

Rainforest Bungalow

Each of our Rainforest Bungalows has been created to offer guests the unique opportunity of experiencing rainforest living in the most natural way, while offering a very high standard of comfort and amenities. The Rainforest Bungalow is perfect for families, couples, or single travelers seeking rest and relaxation. You will be thrilled with our large outdoor deck and living space where you can relax in your own Chachagua inspired rocking chair or hammock.

Open the wooden shutters to your jungle world every morning with the excitement and anticipation of what the rainforest paradise may have in store for you that day. Allow yourself to be gently cooled by air conditioning, giving you an authentic eco-experience. Enjoy a refreshing rain shower after engaging in one of our many on-site adventures; then reward yourself with a rainforest-inspired drink while you put your feet up in your outdoor living space.

Suite Size: 1700 square feet
